Mission

The CareCenter mission is to advocate for the promotion of the mental and physical health and development of children and adolescents through policy, practice and research.

The CareCenter also promotes the study, treatment and prevention of mental and emotional disorders and the care and rehabilitation of physicalical disabilities involving children, adolescents and their families with emphasis on collaboration among the professions of child and adolescent psychiatry, psychology, social work, paediatrics, public health, physioterapy, orthopedics, education, social sciences and other relevant disciplines.
